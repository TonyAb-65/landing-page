Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy Last Updated: April 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how Connect Marketing Business Services ("EZY", "we", "us"), a company established under the laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data in accordance with the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law and its Implementing Regulations. 1. Data Controller and EZY's Role EZY operates in two distinct capacities depending on the type of data: • Data Controller: With respect to data of merchants, delivery vendors, and users registered directly on the Platform. • Data Processor: With respect to personal data of merchants' customers that passes through the Platform solely for the purpose of fulfilling delivery orders. EZY does not control this data and does not use it for any other purpose. Legal Entity Details: • Legal Name: Connect Marketing Business Services • Commercial Registration: 1010794663 • Address: Jeddah, Saudi Arabia • Email: customercare@ezydelivery.io 2. Scope This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the EZY platform. 3. Personal Data We Collect We may collect the following categories of personal data: • Identification and contact details, such as name, phone number, email address, and ID number (where required) • Business information, including commercial records and licenses • Order and delivery details • Payment transaction details processed through licensed payment service providers. EZY maintains an internal settlement ledger used solely for financial reconciliation — EZY does not store payment card data • Technical data, such as IP address, device information, and system logs • Records of communications and support interactions We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data except where required by law and with your explicit consent. 4. Legal Basis for Processing We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds under Article 8 of the Personal Data Protection Law: • Your explicit consent • Performance of a contract with you — the primary basis for processing order and delivery data • Compliance with legal obligations — including anti-money laundering and ZATCA requirements • Our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving our platform Marketing consent is collected separately and optionally and is not bundled with service consent. 5. Purposes of Processing Your personal data is processed for the following purposes: • To create and manage user accounts • To facilitate coordination of delivery requests between merchants and independent delivery service providers through the EZY technology platform • To provide customer support and communications • To enhance and secure our services • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements • To prevent fraud and misuse of our services 6. Disclosure of Data We may share your personal data with the following parties, for specific purposes only: • Independent third-party delivery service providers operating through the EZY platform — solely for the purpose of fulfilling delivery orders • Licensed payment service providers— solely for payment processing and financial settlement • IT and cloud infrastructure providers — contractually bound to the same protection standards • Regulators and governmental authorities, where required by law We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for commercial purposes. 7. Merchant Customer Data EZY may receive personal data of merchants' customers — such as the customer's name, phone number, and delivery address — from merchants registered on the Platform, solely for the purpose of fulfilling delivery requests. In such cases: • The merchant acts as the data controller for their customers' data and bears full responsibility for the lawful basis of collecting and processing such data and for providing any required notices under the Personal Data Protection Law; • EZY acts solely as a data processor — using the data to fulfill the delivery and sharing it with the appointed delivery service provider for that purpose only; • EZY does not use merchant customers' data for any purpose other than fulfilling the delivery order. The legal basis for this processing is contractual necessity under Article 8 of the Personal Data Protection Law, as this data is necessary to execute the delivery contract entered into by the customer with the merchant. 8. Cross-Border Transfers Your personal data may be transferred outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia only in the following circumstances, in accordance with the Data Transfer Regulation issued by the Saudi Data and Artificial Intelligence Authority: • Transfer to countries that provide an adequate level of protection as recognized by the competent authority • Application of appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the Saudi Data and Artificial Intelligence Authority • With your explicit consent in specific cases 9. Data Localization We primarily store personal data within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Data is not processed outside the Kingdom unless the legal conditions set out in Article 7 above are fully met. 10. Data Retention Personal data is retained for the following specific periods: • Active merchant data: for the duration of the contract and for such period thereafter as required by applicable law and regulations • Customer delivery data: for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes of delivery and in accordance with applicable regulatory requirements • Financial records and settlement ledgers: ten (10) years in accordance with ZATCA requirements • Anti-money laundering and identity verification records: ten (10) years in accordance with AML regulations • Dispute and legal claim records: for the period prescribed by the applicable statute of limitations under Saudi law Data is deleted or anonymized upon the expiry of the applicable retention period. 11. Security Measures We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data, including: • Encryption in transit and at rest • Access controls and audit logs • Periodic risk assessments • Employee training on data protection • Access limited on a need-to-know basis 12. Personal Data Breach In the event of a personal data breach: • EZY will notify the Saudi Data and Artificial Intelligence Authority within the timeframe prescribed by the Personal Data Protection Law upon becoming aware of any breach likely to result in a risk to data subjects • Affected individuals will be notified as soon as reasonably practicable, with details of the breach and the measures taken • All breach incidents are documented internally regardless of risk level 13. Data Subject Rights You have the following rights under the Personal Data Protection Law: • Access your personal data • Request correction of inaccurate data • Request deletion of your data where the purpose for processing no longer exists • Withdraw your consent at any time without retroactive effect • Object to the processing of your data • Obtain a copy of your data in a commonly used electronic format To exercise these rights, contact us at: customercare@ezydelivery.io Account Closure: Any user may request account closure at any time. Upon closure, access to the account is immediately and permanently revoked. To resume using the Platform, the user must register a new account using a different email address. EZY retains financial transaction records and order history for the period prescribed by applicable Saudi regulations. Such retention does not constitute a violation of the right to erasure under the Personal Data Protection Law, as it is based on mandatory legal obligations. EZY will endeavor to respond to your requests as soon as reasonably practicable and in accordance with the timelines prescribed by the Personal Data Protection Law and its Implementing Regulations. 14. Right to Lodge a Complaint If you have concerns about how your personal data is handled, you may lodge a complaint with the Saudi Data and Artificial Intelligence Authority through its official website. 15. Children's Data Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not intentionally collect data from minors. If we become aware that data from a minor has been collected unintentionally, it will be deleted immediately. 16. Automated Processing We may utilize automated processing for specific operational purposes such as fraud prevention and service improvement. No decisions with legal or significant effects on individuals are made through fully automated means without human review. 17. Consent Management Explicit consent is obtained separately for each purpose where required. You may withdraw your consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing. Marketing consent is entirely optional and separate from service consent. 18. Changes to this Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes, EZY will endeavor to provide registered users with reasonable advance notice via email or through the Platform prior to the change taking effect. Continued use of the Platform following publication of updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy. 19. Cookie Policy The EZY platform uses cookies as follows: • Essential cookies: necessary for platform operation and do not require prior consent • Analytics and performance cookies: require your explicit consent and can be managed through your browser settings • Marketing cookies: entirely optional and require separate consent 20.
