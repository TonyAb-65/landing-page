EZY streamlines the delivery process for businesses, making logistics and payment management effortless after a sale is completed. Whether you sell through your own channels or online platforms, EZY handles delivery execution so you can focus on sales.

What is EZY?

EZY is a comprehensive logistics technology platform that connects merchants with a network of approved last-mile delivery partners and payment services. With a single, unified platform, merchants submit delivery requests and trust EZY to coordinate the entire process from start to finish. Once you submit a delivery request, EZY orchestrates every step that follows, ensuring smooth and reliable fulfillment.

How EZY Works

You submit a delivery request. EZY routes it to an approved delivery partner, tracks execution in real time, and supports cash on delivery and digital payments. Settlements and reporting are centralized so operations stay clean and predictable.

Why EZY?

EZY simplifies delivery and payment logistics into one streamlined platform, adaptable for every business size and commerce model.
