EZY streamlines the delivery process for businesses, making logistics and payment management effortless after a sale is completed. Whether you sell through your own channels or online platforms, EZY handles delivery execution so you can focus on sales.

What is EZY?

EZY is a comprehensive logistics technology platform that connects merchants with a network of approved last-mile delivery partners and payment services. With a single, unified platform, merchants submit delivery requests and trust EZY to coordinate the entire process from start to finish. Once you submit a request, EZY orchestrates every step that follows, ensuring smooth and reliable fulfillment.

What EZY Provides

  • Centralized management for all delivery requests
  • Access to a variety of delivery vendors
  • Website and API integration options
  • Built-in product catalog for merchants without e-commerce platforms
  • Support for both cash on delivery (COD) and digital payments
  • Real-time tracking of deliveries
  • Automated deductions of applicable fees
  • Wallet feature with weekly settlements
  • Comprehensive delivery history and detailed reporting

How EZY Works

  1. 1
    A sale happens Customers purchase via website, social channels, chat, or in-store.
  2. 2
    A request reaches EZY Requests arrive automatically from your store, or you create them from EZY’s catalog.
  3. 3
    Submit Once delivery details are submitted, EZY initiates execution.
  4. 4
    EZY executes Assigns the most suitable delivery partner, supports COD & online payments, and provides live tracking until completion.
  5. 5
    Settlement Net earnings are credited to your EZY wallet, processed weekly with transparent reporting.

Built for Any Commerce Model

EZY supports a wide range of business models — online stores and brands, custom websites, social commerce, and physical retailers. If your business generates sales, EZY can handle delivery execution with ease.

Why EZY?

EZY simplifies delivery and payment logistics into one streamlined platform, adaptable for every business size.
