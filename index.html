EZY streamlines the delivery process for businesses, making logistics and payment management effortless after a sale is completed. Whether you sell through your own channels or online platforms, EZY takes care of delivery execution so you can focus on sales.

What is EZY?

EZY is a comprehensive logistics technology platform that connects merchants with a network of approved last-mile delivery partners and payment services. With a single, unified platform, merchants can submit delivery requests and trust EZY to coordinate the entire process from start to finish.

Once you submit a delivery request, EZY orchestrates every step that follows, ensuring smooth and reliable fulfillment.

How EZY Works

  • A sale occurs: Customers make purchases on your website, social channels, chat, or in your physical store.
  • A delivery request is sent to EZY: If you have an e-commerce website, requests are generated automatically with items and address. If you don’t, use EZY’s built-in catalog to select items and quantities.
  • Submit the request: Once delivery details are submitted, EZY initiates execution.
  • EZY manages execution: Assigns the most suitable partner, supports COD and digital payments, and provides real-time tracking.
  • Settlement: Net earnings are credited to your EZY wallet with weekly settlements.

What EZY Provides

  • Centralized management for all delivery requests
  • Access to a variety of delivery vendors
  • Website and API integration options
  • Built-in product catalog for merchants without e-commerce platforms
  • Support for both cash on delivery (COD) and digital payments
  • Real-time tracking of deliveries
  • Automated deductions of applicable fees
  • Wallet feature with weekly settlements
  • Comprehensive delivery history and detailed reporting

Built for Any Commerce Model

EZY is designed to support a wide range of business models, including online stores, custom websites, social commerce, brick-and-mortar retailers, and high-volume operations. If your business generates sales, EZY is equipped to handle your delivery needs with ease.

Why EZY?

EZY simplifies delivery and payment logistics into one streamlined platform, adaptable for every business size.
