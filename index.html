What is EZY?

A technology platform — not a delivery company

EZY connects merchants to a network of approved delivery vendors and handles the entire technology layer: payment links, live tracking, reporting, and settlement. The physical delivery is done by our contracted vendor partners — you simply submit the request and EZY coordinates everything.

How EZY works

From sale → execution → settlement

1
A sale occurs

Customer purchases via your website, social channels, WhatsApp, or in-store — however you currently sell.

2
Submit a delivery request

Use EZY's built-in product catalogue to select items and quantities. Weight, price, and total are calculated automatically.

3
EZY handles everything

Payment link sent to customer. Tracking link sent to merchant and customer. Delivery request pushed to the assigned vendor. All automatic — you're notified who handles it.

4
Settlement to your wallet

Collection lands in your EZY wallet. Request a withdrawal and EZY instructs the payment partner to deposit directly to your bank account.
