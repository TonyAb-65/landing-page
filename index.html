What is EZY?

A technology platform — not a delivery company

EZY connects merchants to a network of approved delivery vendors and handles the entire technology layer: payment links, live tracking, reporting, and settlement. The physical delivery is done by our contracted vendor partners — you simply submit the request and EZY coordinates everything.
