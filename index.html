EZY streamlines the delivery process for businesses, making logistics and payment management effortless after a sale is completed. Whether you sell through your own channels or online platforms, EZY takes care of delivery execution so you can focus on sales.

What is EZY?

EZY is a comprehensive logistics technology platform that connects merchants with a network of approved last-mile delivery partners and payment services. With a single, unified platform, merchants can submit delivery requests and trust EZY to coordinate the entire process from start to finish. Once you submit a delivery request, EZY orchestrates every step that follows, ensuring smooth and reliable fulfillment.

How EZY Works

1

A sale occurs

Customers make purchases on your website, social channels, chat, or in your physical store.

2

A delivery request is sent to EZY

If you have an e-commerce website, delivery requests are generated automatically, complete with items and customer address. If you do not have an e-commerce platform, you can use EZY’s built-in product catalog to select items and quantities for delivery.

3

Submit the request

Once your delivery details are submitted, EZY initiates the delivery execution process.

4

EZY manages execution

Assigns the most suitable delivery partner for each request, handles both cash on delivery (COD) and digital payments, provides real-time tracking, and monitors the delivery until it is completed.

5

Settlement

Your net earnings are credited to your EZY wallet. Settlements are processed weekly, with full transparency into all transactions.

What EZY Provides

    EZY is designed to support a wide range of business models, including online stores, custom websites, social commerce, brick-and-mortar retailers, and high-volume operations. If your business generates sales, EZY is equipped to handle your delivery needs with ease.

    EZY simplifies delivery and payment logistics into one streamlined platform, adaptable for every business size.
