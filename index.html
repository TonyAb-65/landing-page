EZY streamlines the delivery process for businesses, making logistics and payment management effortless after a sale is completed. Whether you sell through your own channels or online platforms, EZY handles delivery execution so you can focus on sales.

What is EZY?

EZY is a comprehensive logistics technology platform that connects merchants with a network of approved last-mile delivery partners and payment services. With a single, unified platform, merchants submit delivery requests and trust EZY to coordinate the entire process from start to finish.

What EZY Provides

  • Centralized management for all delivery requests
  • Access to a variety of delivery vendors
  • Website and API integration options
  • Support for both cash on delivery and digital payments
  • Real-time tracking of deliveries
  • Wallet feature with weekly settlements

How EZY Works

  • A sale happens
  • A request reaches EZY
  • Submit delivery request
  • EZY handles execution
  • Settlement

Why EZY?

EZY simplifies delivery and payment logistics into one streamlined platform, adaptable for every business size.

  • One dashboard for requests, statuses, and history
  • Weekly settlements via wallet
  • Transparent fees & automated deductions
  • Designed for online stores, social commerce, and retail
